Dialer Types Explained

Sales Loft uses power dialing (sequential). Here's what exists in 2026:

1. Power Dialing (Sequential)

Agent queues numbers. Dialer calls one-by-one. Best for AEs, high-intent leads. Platforms: Salesloft, Apollo, Revenue.io, Close, Outreach, Freshsales.

2. Parallel Dialing (Predictive)

Dials 3–9 simultaneously. AI detects live answers. 3x more conversations/hour. Platforms: Nooks.ai, Outreach, Orum, Outplay.

3. Preview Dialing

Agent sees prospect info before dialing. Personalization built-in. Quality over speed. Platforms: Talkdesk, some enterprise dialers.

4. Manual Dialing (No Dialer)

Reps manually click to dial. No automation. Platforms: Mixmax, Streak, Gmail-native CRMs.
